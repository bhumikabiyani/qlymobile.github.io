White Tilez

White Tilez is a super fun and highly addictive game suited for everyone.

No special skills needed, all this game asks of you is an attentive mind and fast fingers!

This game is easy, do not touch the white tiles! Touch the black tiles in order to move up to the next round.

Classic Mode:

See how fast you can clear 50 rows. We keep your best time.

Arcade Mode:

The board keeps moving, but how many levels can you clear? We keep the best round.
