Elemental Magic

Elemental Magic is a Roguelike RPG puzzle game featuring the pixel art of the 80s.

You control a mage of the four elements.

Your quest is to defeat the skeleton king of Amun-Argul by using your special magic.

Dwell deeper into the dungeons and defeat the monsters guarding the king.

Connect at least 3 mana of the same type to cast a spell.

Fire damages the monsters.

Water gives you protection.

Earth paralyze the enemies.

Air disintegrates the doors.

The effect of the spell will be more powerful if you connect more mana.

Explore the dungeon to find gold and magic scrolls.

If you use gold, you can connect 2 mana to cast a spell.

If you use a scroll, you can use all mana of the same type you select to cast the spell of the ancients.
